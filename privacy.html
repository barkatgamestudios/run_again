Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Run Again app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, developed by Barkat Game Studios (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a free service. This service is provided "AS IS."

What Information Does the Application Obtain and How Is It Used?

User-Provided Information

The Application does not require you to register or provide personal information. No personal information is collected through the app unless you voluntarily provide it, for example, through contacting support. Some features may be available without registration.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application may automatically collect basic information, such as device type, operating system, and usage statistics, but this data is anonymized and not linked to personal information.

Does the Application Collect Precise Real-Time Location Information of the Device?

This Application does not gather precise location information about your mobile device.

Does the Application Serve Personalized Ads?

The Application does not use personal data for targeting ads. All ads displayed are non-personalized, meaning they are not based on individual user behavior or interests. These ads are shown to all users, regardless of age.

What Does "Non-Personalized" Mean?

Non-personalized ads are ads that do not use any information collected from your device or activity to target you specifically. The ads are based on the content of the app, not on individual user data.

How Do We Ensure Non-Personalized Ads?

  • The application uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK, configured to serve non-personalized ads with the `"npa": "1"` flag.
  • The ads shown are rated G to ensure they are appropriate for all users, including children.
  • The application also sets the **MaxAdContentRating** to **G** and marks the app for **child-directed treatment** to comply with privacy guidelines.

Do Third Parties See and/or Have Access to Information Obtained by the Application?

The Application may share anonymized and aggregated data with third-party service providers, such as Google and Unity, for the purpose of improving the app and serving non-personalized ads. These third-party providers are not allowed to collect personal information without your consent. Please review their privacy policies for more details:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Unity

Children

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The Application does not collect any data that could be used to identify users, including children. If you believe a child under the age of 13 has provided any personal information, please contact us at contact.barkatgs@gmail.com, and we will take immediate action to remove the information.

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

The Service Provider does not retain any personally identifiable information. Any data collected is anonymized and used solely for improving the performance of the Application. If you wish to stop data collection, simply uninstall the Application.

Security

We are committed to safeguarding the security of your information. However, since no personal data is collected or stored, we primarily focus on ensuring the smooth and secure operation of the Application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
